Buldak

Buldak, which means “fire chicken” in Korean, is a spicy Korean dish known for its intense heat and bold flavor. It's made with: Chicken pieces, usually boneless, a fiery sauce made from gochugaru (Korean chili flakes), gochujang (chili paste), soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and sugar. Often grilled or stir-fried. Sometimes topped with melted cheese to balance the heat.
