Buldak Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Buldak Ramen (e.g., Samyang Hot Chicken Flavor)
  • 2 cups water
  • Optional toppings:
  • 1 fried or boiled egg
  • Green onions (sliced)
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toasted sesame seeds
  • Seaweed flakes

  • 1 pack of Buldak Ramen (e.g., Samyang Hot Chicken Flavor)
  • 2 cups water
  • Optional toppings:
  • 1 fried or boiled egg
  • Green onions (sliced)
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toasted sesame seeds
  • Seaweed flakes

Instructions

  • Boil water (about 2 cups) in a pot.
  • Add the noodles and cook for about 4-5 minutes until they are soft.
  • Drain the water, leaving about 2 tablespoons in the pot (very little water).
  • Add the liquid sauce packet and stir-fry the noodles for about 30 seconds.
  • Add the flakes (usually sesame + seaweed) and mix well.
  • Optional: Top with cheese, egg, green onion, etc.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your spicy Buldak Ramen!
